Methodology - Agile

We embrace and adhere to the Agile development methodology. Agile in a nutshell, is:

  • Work with dedicated user from the client to determine requirements (stories).
  • Define what we know and what we know we don't know (spiking).
  • Kick off project with pre-determined stories for the next period of time (sprint).

And, what's the big deal? The big deal is that as developers, we are constantly building software in an interative fashion (lather, rinse, repeat) and it works, very well. Check out this:   Scrum Guide
